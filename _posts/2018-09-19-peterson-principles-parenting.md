5 rules for parenting, from 12 Rules for Life

1. Limit the rules.
2. Use minimum necessary force.
3. Partner up.
4. Understand your capacity to be harsh, vengeful, arrogant, resentful, angry and deceitful.
5. Act as a proxy for the real world.
